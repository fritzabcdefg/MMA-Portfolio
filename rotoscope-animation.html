> ROTOSCOPE ANIMATION

Frame-by-Frame Artistry

Rotoscoping is a meticulous animation technique that involves tracing over live-action footage, frame by frame. This section showcases my frame-by-frame animation work, featuring detailed roto projects, digital painting, and hand-drawn animations layered over live-action sequences.
