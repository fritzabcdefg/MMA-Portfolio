Rotoscope Tutorial

Follow these tutorial cards for a compact workflow overview, with each image showing the key step in the rotoscope process.

Step 1: Find reference

Choose a clear motion clip that shows the action you want to rotoscope.

Step 2: Trim the clip

Cut the reference into short segments so each frame is easy to trace.

Step 3: Import clip

Create a new composition and bring your trimmed clip into After Effects.

Step 4: Mask the subject

Use the Rotobrush tool to trace and isolate the subject frame by frame.

Step 5: Render clip

Render the masked animation clip once the rotoscope is clean and smooth.

Step 6: Final export

Compile your clips, add effects if needed, then export the final rotoscope animation.
